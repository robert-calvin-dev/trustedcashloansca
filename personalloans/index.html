What Is a Personal Installment Loan?

A personal installment loan is a fixed-sum loan that is repaid over a set number of scheduled payments. Unlike payday loans, which are due in full on your next payday, installment loans allow you to repay gradually, typically over 6 to 72 months.

Common Uses for Installment Loans

  • Debt consolidation
  • Medical expenses
  • Auto repairs or upgrades
  • Unexpected bills or emergencies
  • Major purchases or home improvements

Loan Amounts & Terms

Loan amounts range from $1,000 to $5,000 depending on your creditworthiness and state laws. Repayment terms vary by lender but often range between 6 and 36 months, with monthly payments fixed for the life of the loan.

Why Choose an Installment Loan?

  • Lower, predictable monthly payments
