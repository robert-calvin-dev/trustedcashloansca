Last Updated: May 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Trusted Cash Loans (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you visit TrustedCashLoans.com (the “Site”). By using this Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information from you when you interact with our Site or submit an application form. This may include:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home and Mailing Address
  • Employment and Financial Information
  • IP address and browser details

2. Use of Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To connect you with third-party lenders or financial service providers
  • To personalize and improve your experience on the Site
  • To contact you via email, phone, or SMS based on your application and consent
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party lenders, networks, and financial partners

We use cookies, pixels, and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve Site functionality and deliver personalized experiences. These tools may collect data such as your browser type, visited pages, and device information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion by contacting us directly.

6. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and secure data transmission. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, email: privacy@trustedcashloans.com

Our Site may contain links to external websites not operated by us. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or data handling on these sites.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such data being collected, we will delete it promptly.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the Site constitutes your agreement to the updated terms.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, contact us at:
privacy@trustedcashloans.com
